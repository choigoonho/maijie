LED WALL WASHER LIGHT
   
                                     
      LED WALL WASHER LIGHT is mainly used for decoration and highlight in building frame, garden, restaurant and architectural lighting etc, by offering colorful and magic environment. It can work in independent mode and DMX mode, which can generate various color changing effects, such as flashing, fading, steady, seven colour jumping synchronously etc. DMX 256 classes gray degree dimmer, DMX programmable (thousands of effects available if many lights connected together).
